The People at The DailyDATA

Alisha runs The DailyDATA on her own as a personal project.

She is passionate about turning the vast amounts of data that exists into visualizations that are clear and understandable, and enjoys exploring the stories that data can tell.

Alisha is always curious, asking questions, and finding new ways to learn from the data around her. Keep visiting The DailyDATA and learn along with her!
