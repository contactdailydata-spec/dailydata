Global Coffee Trade Flows of Top Exporters to Key Importers

2025-09-15

Did you know that among the top exporters of coffee beans in 2023 Brazil sent the largest shipments especially to the US followed by Vietnam and Colombia?



Sankey diagram showing coffee trade flows (in million kilograms) from top exporters to selected major importers in 2023. Flow widths are proportional to trade weight. Only the largest exporters and major importers were included in the calculations to keep the figure clear and focus on key trade routes.


Data source: Created using data from UN Comtrade (2023).
