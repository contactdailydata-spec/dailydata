Titanic Survivors by Class and Gender

2025-10-21

Did you know that men had the lowest survival rate in the Titanic? This clearly shows the women and children first policy.



The data for this plot comes from the titanic R package which provides a cleaned version of the historical Titanic passenger dataset. We use the titanic_train dataset which includes 891 passengers from the RMS Titanic’s voyage in 1912.



*These figures have not yet been formally peer reviewed and are intended as exploratory
